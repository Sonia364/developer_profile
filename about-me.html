About Sonia Nain

Hi, I’m Sonia, a Full Stack Web Developer specializing in WordPress and WooCommerce development with a strong focus on building scalable, performance-driven web applications. I have hands-on experience in custom plugin development, theme customization, and complex eCommerce solutions using PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

I started my career at Deftsoft as a Software Engineer, where I worked on diverse web applications using technologies like Laravel, Magento, and WordPress. I later joined Fresco Web Services as a Senior Web Developer, where I built and maintained WordPress Multisite solutions, integrated third-party APIs, and optimized database performance for better efficiency.

Currently, I work as a Web Developer at Atlas Lighting and Sales Ltd, focusing on WooCommerce customization, ERP integrations, dynamic pricing systems, and advanced eCommerce features like rebate logic, shipping insurance, and secure payment workflows.

I enjoy solving complex technical problems and building user-focused digital experiences.

In my free time, I enjoy working out and sketching.
