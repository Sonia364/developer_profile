About Sonia Nain

Hi, I'm Sonia! I am a web developer having a passion for creating websites and mobile applications that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Application degree from MCM DAV College in Chandigarh, India in 2017. I am currently pursuing a Mobile Application Design and Development program at Lambton College in Toronto, Canada.

I started my professional career as a Jr. Web Developer at Deftsoft, where I worked from 2018 to 2021. During my time there, I gained experience in developing and maintaining websites for various clients. In 2021, I joined Fresco as a Sr. Web Developer, where I am currently working on building web applications using the latest technologies.

In my current role as a Senior Web Developer at Fresco Web Services Pvt. Ltd, I've had the privilege of honing my craft. I am adept at programming languages such as PHP, MySQL, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, XML/JSON, and have seamlessly integrated 3rd party APIs into projects. Moreover, I have a proven track record of tailoring WordPress plugins and themes through adept use of PHP and WordPress APIs.

In my free time, I enjoy working out and sketching.
