Atlas Lighting & Sales Ltd : Major Areas Of Work

Website Redesign & Responsiveness (WordPress)

Redesigned and restructured the entire website, including WooCommerce pages (product, cart, empty cart, checkout) and email templates, to improve layout and navigation flow. Implemented a fully responsive design across all devices, ensuring consistency and better usability throughout the site. Added a disclaimer prompt before add-to-cart to inform customers about harmful UV radiation emitted by certain lamps.

IESO Rebate Functionality (WooCommerce)

Implemented location-based rebate functionality for Ontario users, displaying eligible products and triggering a rebate modal on product and brand pages based on IP detection. Customers can check eligibility and accept or decline the rebate, with discounts automatically applied to the cart upon acceptance. Post-checkout, a form is generated for completion and signature, with a PDF copy provided to the customer.

Shipping Insurance Feature (WooCommerce)

Implemented a shipping insurance feature allowing customers to add or remove coverage directly from the cart/checkout. Built using core PHP, WooCommerce hooks, and front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, jQuery) to ensure seamless integration with the existing purchase flow.

Custom Quantity-Based Pricing (WooCommerce)

Implemented custom pricing logic based on product quantity, allowing customers to enter quantities and view dynamic discounted pricing. This improves pricing transparency and helps users make better purchase decisions directly on the product page.
